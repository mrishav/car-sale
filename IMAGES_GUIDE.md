# Adding Photos to Your McLaren 570S Site

## Quick Start

Add your car photos to the folder:
```
/public/images/car/
```

## Recommended Photos

For the best presentation, include these shots:

### Exterior Photos
- **Front view** - Showcase the aggressive front fascia
- **Side profile** - Highlight the iconic McLaren silhouette
- **Rear view** - Show the distinctive rear diffuser and exhaust
- **3/4 front angle** - Best overall view
- **3/4 rear angle** - Complete the angles

### Interior Photos
- **Dashboard** - Full view of the driver's cockpit
- **Seats** - Show the carbon fiber interior upgrade
- **Center console** - Detail the luxury features
- **Steering wheel** - McLaren badge and controls
- **Passenger side** - Complete interior view

### Detail Shots
- **Engine bay** - Show the aluminum panels
- **Wheels** - Close-up of McLaren Orange calipers
- **Carbon fiber details** - Interior upgrades
- **Badges** - Stealth badging
- **Exhaust** - Sports exhaust system
- **Door sills** - McLaren branding
- **PPF coverage** - Show the protection quality

## Image Tips

- **Resolution**: Use high-resolution images (at least 1920x1080)
- **Lighting**: Take photos in good natural light
- **Clean**: Ensure the car is clean and detailed
- **Background**: Use clean, uncluttered backgrounds
- **Format**: JPG or PNG format works best
- **File size**: Optimize images (under 2MB each for web performance)

## File Naming

Use descriptive names like:
- `exterior-front.jpg`
- `interior-dashboard.jpg`
- `detail-wheels.jpg`
- `engine-bay.jpg`

## Next Steps

Once you add photos, you can update the Gallery section in `src/App.jsx` to display them automatically or use a simple image gallery component.
